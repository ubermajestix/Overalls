JQuery.Overalls: Easily Customizable Overlays

Overlays Lightboxes Dialogs Oh My!

This plugin is for developers and designers who want precise control over their overlays/lightboxs/dialogs.

Lots of plugins out there make you use their design and add a ton of html to the page making customization not easy and brittle.

Overalls is simple: it places one huge semi-transparent div over the whole page and one div (overalls-overlay) to the page. Design the overall-overlay div in your css, add your html and whaala overlay goodness customized.

Opening the overlay and putting html in it.

Overalls adds a few interaction controls to get you going.

First, when you open the overlay you will get a click handler that close the overlay when you click on the transparent div or hit the ESC key.
